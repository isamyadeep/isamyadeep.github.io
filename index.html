How It Works

Step 1

We begin with a quick call or through your preferred communication app, where you share your business needs, setting the foundation for our collaboration.

Step 2

After our initial discussion, I'll promptly start on your project, providing regular updates. Our payment terms involve an upfront payment, ensuring a smooth and transparent workflow.

Step 3

Your custom website design or completed website is ready for review. Upon completing the remaining payment (if any), you'll receive the files, as outlined in our agreement, ensuring a seamless process.
